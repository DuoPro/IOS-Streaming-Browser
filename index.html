IOS-Streaming-Browser by rubelw

An embedded HTTP for iPhone or iPad which steams the view in 1 second intervals. This allows users to conduct presentations, or allows others to watch what you are doing on a screen. It is designed to allow teachers to view student activity, or to allow students to give presentations from their iPads

Install

Compile with xcode4 with iOS 4.3
